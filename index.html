WELL EXPERIENCED

I started in business 15 years ago. The main of the time in last decade I have focused on waterproofing business being employed by one of the largest producer of waterproofing membranes in Europe. My tasks were to establish business partnerships across Europe. I started almost from zero.
Later, when the business in Europe was well launched and developing, I concentrated on countries outside Europe. China, Turkey, India, Australia, UAE. Another new customers gained.
During the time I collected lots of experiences across the industry, I met great professionals and enjoyed travelling. I learnt where is who active, who is what produces, who can supply what, who can have an interest for what material.
